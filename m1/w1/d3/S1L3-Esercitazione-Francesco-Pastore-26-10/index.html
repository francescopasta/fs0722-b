Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspons of salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lokewater water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 table spoon for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie into cubes(scrape and rip off parking the knife with the cisarcs, more than ncessary)
  • 11/2 cups of fresh or frozen cranberries
